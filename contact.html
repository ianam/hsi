About Us

At Health Staffing International, we help facilities find nursing professionals and help nursing professionals diversify their careers. Incorporated in BC in 1997, we are one of the longest-serving healthcare staffing companies in the province. Over the last 3 decades we have established a large network of healthcare facilities and nursing professionals. We are well acquainted with nursing labour relations, quality assurance standards, facility funding structures and regulatory requirements. Health Staffing International knows the demands placed on staff and on facility operators. We provide a comprehensive gateway for all your healthcare needs.
