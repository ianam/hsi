    • Our Services

    We know the demands placed on staff and facility operators, and provide a full range of services to fill openings, improve efficiency and labour relations for you and your staff. Our approach emphasizes efficiency, professionalism, and quality assurance.

    Call us today for all your staffing needs

    We are open 7 days a week, including holidays

    Cost savings

    Reduce your financial pressures and significant overtime costs associate with staffing shortages. Our flexible services will save you money on overtime with little to no administrative fees. We cover the costs of orientation so both staff and employer are confident and prepared for short-term coverage.

    Rapid response

    Our scheduling department is open 7 days a week, including weekends and holidays, to fill immediate staff vacancies in your facility.

    Flexible contracts

    Short and long-term assistance maximizes efficiency and flexibility in staffing. Cover immediate staffing needs due to sick leave or vacation, or long-term blocks such as sabbaticals, maternity leave, and manager or director of care interim coverage.

    Staff relief

    Maintain worker morale, accuracy, and safety by reducing your overtime rate ratio with our staff placements. We assist in providing relief and preventing crisis situations relating to staff shortages and unexpected circumstances.

    Union friendly

    We support nursing unions, working alongside the BCNU for over two decades. Our staff supports the working environment for health workers, and an equivalent amount to union dues is paid by each health region to the union for hours worked by our nurses.
