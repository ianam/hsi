    • Our Staff

    Each of our employees are screened to ensure that they meet the standards and licensing criteria for employment, as well as their compatibility with the healthcare environment. Our goal is to seamlessly integrate the highest quality professionals into your facility, helping you maintain a high standard of care.

    Try before you buy

    Our client satisfaction with our staff is of the utmost importance. Facilities can screen staff to ensure compatibility, and in instances where they aren't the right fit can request replacements without concerns surrounding labour relations, arbitration, or wrongful dismissal.

    Quality Standards

    All of our staff meet provincial licensing criteria, and go through criminal records checks, immunization and TB screening, current First Aid/CPR, and hold practicing registration with applicable provincial professional bodies. We check a minimum of three references for each short-listed applicant.

    Diversity

    We work with highly motivated, extremely adaptable, and intelligent professionals from a variety of backgrounds and designations. Our staff has experience in acute hospitals, multi-level care facilities, extended care units, psychiatric programs and residential settings. As diverse as the population they serve, they meet most language requirements for care.

    Nursing Supervision

    Our nursing directors offer individualized, two-level supervisory service. The staff level prepares workers for each job via orientation and location-specific details. The managerial level provides an open line of communication to provide the most comprehensive response and assistance with any potential issues.
