Professionalism

Experience to cover all your needs

Industry Experience: Our 30 years of industry experience give us the knowledge to support your organization, with experience in a range of public and private instutions and centres in all the major health regions of British Columbia. Strong client base rewrite

Diverse Workforce: Our staff of highly motivated, adaptable and intelligent professionals have experience in acute hospitals, multi-level care facilities, extended care units, psychiatric programs, and residential settings. Our staff diversity includes ties to many global regions, and cover most language requirements to meet the needs of the various groups we serve.

Union Friendly: Our company has worked alongside the BCNU for over two decades without dispute. Our purpose is not to replace union jobs, but rather support the working environment of union staff by reducing burnout and stress. An amount equivalent to union dues is paid for hours worked by our nurses in each health region.
