Employment

Our company's goal is to provide a nursing gateway to the world by offering nurses the best career opportunities in hospital, geriatric, or community settings. Our strength is our people. Our company boasts a diverse multi-cultural complement of staff with ties to each of the major continents of the world. We will work with you to build your career while offering you personalized flexibility to suit your work-life balance needs.

What We Offer

Flexibility

Every employee is in complete control of their own work schedule. Choose the days you work, and change your schedule at will. For part-time placements, our scheduling process allows you to fill openings without interrupting your current rotation. Our assignments are available for every day and time slot, singly or in blocks.

Variety & Versatility

Our nurses work in a variety of settings, gaining valuable exposure to different types of health care institutions to better plan their long-term careers. We have opportunities across the major health regions of British Columbia in:

Acute Care OR Cardiac
Emergency Maternity Psychiatric
Multi-Level Care Extended Care Private Hospitals
Home Support Rehabilitation Centres Private Surgical Centres
Group Homes Special Care Units Seniors' Centres

Higher Wages

We fill vacancies that would otherwise be filled by the hospital requiring one of their own staff to work at overtime rates, so we are able to offer more competitive rates than long-term care facilities or hospitals. We also choose to pay higher wages to our nurses instead of maintaining costly accumulations such as compassionate leave credits. Additionally, you can choose to work as a contractor to gain significant advantages under Canadian tax laws.

Less Stress

Our staff are bound only to the schedule they know they can handle from week to week. They are not forced to work overtime when they are exhausted. They can alter their work availability on a daily basis so as to better fit with their personal needs. All staff are given on-the-job introduction and training to assist them in adjusting to the systems and routines of each institution.

No Politics

In instances of conflicting interests between union and employer or facility, our employees do not have to choose sides, and always have the options of changing their job site. In unionized work-stoppages we can cross picket lines because we are assisting the union in meeting their requirements under essential services legislation. We are not taking away union jobs, we are only filling in after the union resources are exhausted.
