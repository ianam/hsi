Staffing Standards

Reduce risk and worry

Each of our employees are screened to ensure that they have met provincial licensing criteria, including Criminal Record checks, immunization and TB screening, current First Aid/CPR and practicing registration with applicable provincial professional bodies. We check a minimum of three references for each short-listed applicant, and evaluate each staff member's comptability with the healthcare environment. Workers Compensation membership is held in good standing with coverage of all premiums for staff, and we maintain comprehensive commercial and professional liability insurance coverage.
