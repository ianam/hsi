    Critical Care Nurses

    Health Staffing is hiring critical care and ICU nurses for immediate openings in the BC Lower Mainland.

    Health Staffing International is looking for skilled Critical Care Nurses to work immediately in the BC Lower Mainland. Rate up to $60/hr based on experience. Must have current practicing registration with the British Columbia College of Nurses & Midwives (BCCNM), along with current CPR (BCLS)/First Aide certificates. Current Advanced Cardiac Life Support (ACLS) Certificate. Ability to assess and manage critical and life-threatening situations. Must be up to date with immunizations/ TB test, and include two written professional references.

    Position requires completion of a recognized critical care nursing program or two years' recent, related critical care experience. Knowledge of the physiological and behavioural aspects of complex critical illnesses and nursing problems commonly encountered in a critical care environment.

    Skills Include:

    • Knowledge of BCCNM's standards for nursing practice, knowledge of other health care disciplines and their role in client care.
    • Thorough knowledge of the indications, actions, reactions, routes, dosages, and side effects of anaesthetic and other drugs used in critical care environment.
    • Skill in techniques appropriate to a critical care environment, skilled in the use of critical care monitoring and support equipment.
    • Ability to respond immediately and appropriately to sudden emergencies.
    • Ability to organize and prioritize workload, work collaboratively as a member of an interdisciplinary team, ability to plan and implement plans of care.
    • Ability to communicate with, and deal effectively with, clients and their families, co-workers, physicians, and other health care staff, and teach clients and their families.
    • Ability to communicate in writing accurately, concisely, and clearly along with basic computer literacy to operate a computerized patient care information system.

    Requirements:

    • Graduation from an approved school of Nursing
    • Current registration with the British Columbia College of Nurses & Midwives (BCCNM)
    • Post basic Critical Care education
    • Experience in Critical Care Nursing
    • Current Basic Cardiac Life Support (BCLS) Certificate
    • Current Advanced Cardiac Life Support (ACLS) Certificate
