    • Service Area

    We staff public and private facilities across the Greater Vancouver and Fraser Valley region. Over 30 years of operation we have provided staff and support to a wide variety of facilities and institutions.

    Service Area Map

    Health Staffing has staffing opportunities throughout British Columbia, however our primary focus is on services within the Greater Vancouver and Western Fraser Valley region. This includes West Vancouver to Chilliwack and all municipalities in between.

    Our Clients

    Our management team is qualified and experienced in facility and Health Care Agency Administration. Health Staffing ensures that our clients' staffing needs are supported by properly qualified individuals. Our clients include:

    • Acute Hospitals
    • Multi-level care facilities
    • Extended care units
    • Private hospitals
    • Home support agencies
    • Alcohol & drug rehabilitation centres
    • Private surgical centres
    • Group homes
    • Mental health boarding homes
    • Special care units
  • Seniors' centres
