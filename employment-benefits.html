    • Employment Benefits

    Our goal is to provide you with a nursing gateway by offering the best career opportunities in hospital, geriatric, or community settings. Our strength is our people, and we work with you to build your career while offering the flexibility suited to your individual needs.

    Flexibility

    Each employee is in complete control of their own work schedule. Choose the days your work, and change your schedule according to your needs. Scheduling for part-time placements allows you to fill openings without interrupting your current rotations. Assignments are available for every day and time slot, singly or in blocks.

    Versatility

    Our nurses work in a variety of settings, gaining valuable exposure to different types of health care institutions to better plan their long-term career goals. We have opportunities in public and private institutions across the major health regions in BC.

    Higher Wages

    We fill vacancies that would otherwise be filled by the hospital, requiring overtime rates, and are therefore able to offer more competitive rates than long-term care facilities or hospitals. Working as a contractor has additional significant advantages under Canadian tax laws.

    Less Stress

    Our staff are bound only to the schedule they know they can handle from week to week. There is no mandatory overtime, and work availability can be altered on a daily basis to better fit personal needs. All staff are given on-the-job instruction and training to familiarize them with the systems and routines of each institution.

    No Politics

    In instances of conflicting interests between union and employer or facility, our employees do not have to choose sides, and always have the option of changing their job site. In unionized work-stoppages we can cross picket lines to assist the union in meeting their requirements under essential services legislation.
